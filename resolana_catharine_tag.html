'Resolana' is unfolding through diverse iterations and collaborative processes within the SenseLab, a laboratory for thought in motion at the crossroads of art and philosophy, based in Concordia University, 'where concept-work and relational techniques emerge through live experimentation with new forms of transdisciplinary collaboration' (Massumi, 2016).

This iteration involved a collaboration with Catharine Cary, improvising with projected live coding into materials in movement through a conversational approach between analog (tagging) and digital (typing) writing; previous explorations aroused the creation of a web socket through p5, that receives real-time input through the keyboard and displace the signs with unexpected movements, overlapping and moving between gradients of colors and fluxes.
