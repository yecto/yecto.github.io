La lluvia haciendo música.
                                                      - cafeleería

Musician, visual artist and designer, Rodrigo Velasco «yecto» explores transversal movements of thought and feeling by creating audiovisual works at the crossroads of live coding, jazz guitar and generative design. Improvisation and collaborative processes are at the heart of the experience, where thought is being transformed into abstract worlds of relation. Through workshops and audiovisual performances, his explorations have been shared internationally as part of 'Live Coding Atelier 2018 - 2019' at Eastern Bloc, 'Présence Mexicaine' at TOPO 'Laboratoire d'Ecritures Numériques' - 19e congrès de ELO 2018 - Electronic Literature Organization', 'live => coding music', at 'IMPA' 'Instituto de Matemática Pura e Aplicada - Rio de Janeiro', MUTEK Montréal Edition 19, and /*vivo*/ 2014, 'Simposio Internacional de Música y Código'. Rodrigo is a student of the Master of Design at Concordia University in the Department of Design and Computation Arts and part of the SenseLab, a laboratory for thought in motion.
