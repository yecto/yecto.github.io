México, Ecatepec, Mil988, currently in Montréal.

I studied Graphic Design and Visual Arts, I'm founder of veería a lab focused on web identities, web design and net art.

Around 2011, I involved me in live coding. I'm a visualist projecting algorithmic audioreactive shapes, I'm exploring text as live coded output and I like to explore/create rhythms and sounds, as part of the Algorave movement under the alias yecto.

I'm glad to collaborate and learn from Ernesto Romero, Elamed, Faxtar, Alex McLean, emewe, Arturo Waldo, Joanne Armitage, ANEMONAL, Jason Levine, Marc Matatya, Alexandra Cárdenas, Vieux Néant, Stephanie Castonguay and the inspiring TOPLAP.
